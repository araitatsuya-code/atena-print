Place Japanese TrueType font files here for better PDF rendering:

  IPAGothic.ttf  — sans-serif (gothic), for nameFontFamily: "sans-serif"
  IPAMincho.ttf  — serif (mincho),      for nameFontFamily: "serif"

Download (IPA Font License, free):
  https://moji.or.jp/ipafont/ipafontdownload/

Without these files the app falls back to system fonts (Songti / STHeiti).
